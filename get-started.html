✂ CloverCut · How to Submit

Start in 3 Simple Steps

No complicated forms. No confusing portals. Upload your footage to Google Drive, drop us an email, and we'll handle the rest.

1
Step One
Upload Your Footage to Google Drive
Open Google Drive and upload all your raw video files. This is the easiest way to share large files — no size limits, no compression.
📁 How to upload:
1. Go to drive.google.com and sign in.
2. Click + New → New Folder. Name it something like "CloverCut – [Your Channel]".
3. Open that folder, then drag & drop your video files in (or click + New → File upload).
🔗 Share the folder:
Right-click the folder → Share → under "General access", choose "Anyone with the link" → click Copy link. You'll paste this in the email.
2
Step Two
Compose Your Email
Write to us at clovercut.inc@gmail.com. Paste the Google Drive link and describe what you need.

⚠️ Important: Include a link to a YouTube video that matches the editing style you want. This helps us understand your vision immediately — no guesswork.
To: clovercut.inc@gmail.com Subject: New Editing Request – [Your Channel / Video Title] Hi CloverCut, I'd like to get a video edited. Here are my details: 📁 Footage link: [Paste your Google Drive link here] ✂️ What I need: [e.g. Full edit with captions, music & colour grade] 🎯 Style / Vibe: [e.g. Fast-paced & energetic / Clean and minimal] 🎬 Reference video: [YouTube link — editing style you want us to match] ⏱ Deadline: [e.g. Within 48 hours / No rush] 💬 Extra notes: [Any specific cuts, text, or music preferences] Thanks, [Your Name]
💡 Why include a reference video?
A reference video tells us more about your desired style than a paragraph of description ever could. Link any YouTube video — yours, a competitor's, or any creator whose editing you love.
3
Step Three
Hit Send — We Reply Fast
That's it! We'll review your project and reply within 2 hours (usually much faster) to confirm details, pricing, and your delivery timeline.
⚡ What happens next:
We'll reply with a confirmation and any follow-up questions. Once you approve, we start editing immediately. Your finished video will be delivered via Google Drive, ready to publish.
